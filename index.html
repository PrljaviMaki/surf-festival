Festival Info

Nature meets music

Visitors can spend the day enjoying music and food at the Piknik stage, or join a boat party that returns in time for the main programme at Medane Beach.

Regional meets global

Combining leading acts from Croatia, Serbia and Slovenia with established artists from Europe and beyond.

Sound & community

Crisp sound tuned by Slovenia's Boris Soundsystem. Community-driven workshops, talks and listening sessions.
